What is a rel?

A rel is a subdivision of a day, such that there are 100 rels in a day, or about 4 in an hour. 100 primers is a rel, 100 seconds (rel-seconds) is a primer.

But more importantly, the sunrise is always at 0 rels (or 100 rels), regardless of timezone or time of the year.
